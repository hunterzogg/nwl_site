Team Rankings

Every manager's individual season ranked against every other season in league history.

Methodology: Each row is one manager's single season (not a career average). Each season's league-wide average score is compared against the all-time baseline to produce a year adjustment factor, which corrects for rule changes that shifted scoring up or down league-wide. Both the adjusted and raw (unadjusted) average are shown — adjusted is the default sort, but you can switch to raw at any time.
